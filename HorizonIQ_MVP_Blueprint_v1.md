# HorizonIQ MVP Blueprint v1

## Product Name

HorizonIQ

Tagline:

Observe. Predict. Lead.

---

# Mission

Help students, professionals, and entrepreneurs understand what is changing in the world, why it matters, and what action they should take next.

---

# Target Users

## Student

Needs:

* Career guidance
* Technology awareness
* Skill recommendations

Questions:

* What should I learn?
* What jobs are growing?
* What technologies matter?

---

## Professional

Needs:

* Industry intelligence
* Career resilience
* Skill development

Questions:

* What is changing in my industry?
* What should I learn next?
* What risks are emerging?

---

## Entrepreneur

Needs:

* Opportunity discovery
* Market intelligence
* Emerging trends

Questions:

* What industries are growing?
* What opportunities are emerging?
* What businesses will exist in 5 years?

---

# Dashboard Components

## Top Signals

Display highest momentum trends.

Examples:

* AI Agents
* Robotics
* Digital Twins
* Quantum Computing
* Synthetic Biology

Each signal contains:

* Momentum Score
* Confidence Score
* Summary

---

## Recommended Skills

Personalized recommendations based on:

* User role
* Region
* Interests

---

## Emerging Opportunities

Industry-specific opportunities.

---

## Recommended Actions

The most important section.

Users must leave with clear next steps.

---

# Data Strategy

Phase 1:

Use curated mock datasets.

Files:

signals.json
skills.json
jobs.json
recommendations.json

Phase 2:

Integrate live data sources.

---

# Success Metric

A user should receive useful and actionable intelligence within 60 seconds.

---

# Design Principles

* Clarity over complexity
* Intelligence over decoration
* Premium but minimal
* Mobile first
* Fast and responsive
